Our Expertise

Modular Operation Theatre

Our Modular Operation Theatre is a jointless and seamless structure designed to maintain ultra-clean environments, reducing infection risks during surgeries.

The system provides a 0.3 micron clean air environment with a Vertical Laminar Air Flow system. The jointless structure eliminates 90° corners, preventing bacterial growth.

Hospital Gas Infrastructure

Our MGPS solutions provide safe and reliable medical gas distribution for hospitals, covering oxygen, nitrous oxide, and carbon dioxide systems with certified copper piping and advanced control panels.

All piping uses BS EN 13348:2008 certified, Lloyd certified, medical grade carbon-free copper with Harris USA brazing rods and nitrogen purging.
