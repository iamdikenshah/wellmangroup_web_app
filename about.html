Wellman Group — Your Healthcare Infrastructure Partner

Wellman Group is a multidisciplinary healthcare infrastructure organization established in 2013. We are backed by a team of biomedical engineers and healthcare management professionals with strong experience in the healthcare sector.

We specialize in hospital project planning, modular operation theatre design, medical gas pipeline systems, and clean room facilities. All our installations follow NABH and ISO5 guidelines, ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety.

With a dedicated in-house installation team for modular panels, vinyl flooring, and medical gas pipeline systems, we deliver complete end-to-end solutions.
